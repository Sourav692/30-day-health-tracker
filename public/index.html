Same rule: 5 min movement every hour

📊 How to Score

Score at the end of the day honestly. Look at weekly trends, not single days.

⚡ Energy (1–5)

"How physically and mentally active did I feel today?"

1Very low — tired all day, no motivation
2Low — sluggish, forced yourself
3Average — nothing great or bad
4Good — active, focused, decent stamina
5High — energetic, sharp, motivated

❤️ Libido (1–5)

"How strong was my natural desire today?"

1No desire — no interest at all
2Low — rare thoughts, weak interest
3Moderate — some interest, not strong
4Good — clear desire, responsive
5High — strong desire, strong response
Extra markers to notice: Morning erections (yes/no/strong), ease of erection, spontaneous desire. These tell you more than numbers.
⚠️ Be honest, not optimistic. If you fake higher scores, you lose the whole benefit. Even going from 2 → 3 is real progress.

🔥 Rules You Must NOT Break

  • ❌ No skipping exercise more than 2 days in a row
  • ❌ No all-night scrolling

🧠 What Will Make the Biggest Difference

If you do only 3 things consistently:

  • 1. Exercise
  • 2. Sleep
  • 3. Reduce overstimulation

→ You will see change.

📅 What to Expect

  • Week 1–2 → Slight increase in energy, mood
  • Week 2–3 → Morning erections improve
  • Week 3–4 → Better libido + more consistent performance

Full optimization: 6–12 weeks. But 1 month is enough to feel change.

⚠️ If No Improvement After 4–6 Weeks

Get blood tests for:

  • Vitamin D
  • Testosterone (morning test)
  • Vitamin B12
  • Thyroid (TSH)

  • Avoid junk + excess sugar
